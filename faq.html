Can the teacher edit my points in the assignments I have submitted?

Yes! When the teacher evaluates you once with certain points for the assignment you have submitted for a specific subject , he/she can edit those points if necessary.

Do the Teacher see if we submit the assignment late or after the deadline finish,and what would happen in this case?

Yes! The Teacher can see if you submitted late.In this case It's up to the teacher so he/she can give you penalty points.

Can others see my assignment points?

No! Nobody can see your assignment points,just you and your Teacher.
